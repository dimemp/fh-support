To sign in to your Founders account go to the homepage of www.thefounderhood.com, select Log in in the upper right part of the screen.

Enter your credentials in the form and select log in

To sign out click on the arrow next to the icon in the upper right corner and select sign out

To change basic info of my startup, in my profile select Info

.

Click on the pencil next to the field you want to change and select save to save the changes

Follow the same procedure to change more startup info

To preview my team’s members, in my profile select Team

.

Scroll down to see every member of the team, to invite new members to the team you can send them the "Invite your team members" link

To share your profile select share your profile and then choose one of the available ways

To set interest about collaborations, in my profile select Collaborations

.

Check the button next to the type of collaboration you are interested

Select the pencil next to the field you want to edit and click save to save the changes

To change the password, in my profile select Settings

.

Fill the fields and press change password to save the changes
