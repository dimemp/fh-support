Homepage ▷ Organizers ▷ Organization

•To change your organization name and email, select the drop down menu from the home page and select the demo account under the organizations title. On the new page you are redirected to, you can change your organization name and email. To make the changes, finally click the save button at the bottom of the page. On the same page and with the same method you followed above, you can add or change your short description, your website link, link your linkedin account as well as twitter,facebook and crunchbase.

.

•To invite new members to your organisation, go to your organisation's account and select team

•On the new page you are redirected to, you can copy the URL link and send it to anyone you wish to invite.

•To remove a member from your organization, simply select remove at the bottom of the page in the team section and click on the person you want to remove.
