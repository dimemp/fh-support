Homepage ▷ Organizers ▷ Program

•To change the name of our program, select the founders & organizers button from the home page. On the new page we are taken to and in the customize section, in the field below the Name of the program, we can type the name we want. Finally, at the bottom of the page we click the save button.

•To change the type of our program, we select the drop down menu titled type, and make our choice. Finally, at the bottom of the page we click the save button.

•To select the venture stage where we are, we select the drop down menu with the title Ventures stage and make our selection. Finally, at the bottom of the page we click the save button.

•To add a banner of our choice, in the area titled Banner by clicking we can select the archive we want for banner. Finally, at the bottom of the page we click the save button.

•To change the country of the program, select the drop down menu titled Country, and select the country you want. Finally, at the bottom of the page we click the save button.

•To add or remove the tags of the program, click the arrow below the title Industries looking for and add or remove the tags you want. Finally, at the bottom of the page we click the save button.

•To set organizers in our program, type the organizer you want under the Organizers title. Finally, at the bottom of the page click the save button. To set a deadline for your program, select the desired date under the title Deadline for a venture to join the program or applications. Finally, at the bottom of the page click on the save button.

•To add a short description of our program, on the home page select the founders & organizers button.

•On the new page we were taken to and in the customize section, we select the details button.

•Under the Short description title, type in the text we want and click the save button.

•To set a timetable, under the title, enter the desired data and press the save button.

•To add a short description of what your program is offering, under the title Short description of what your program is offering, type the title you want and press the save button.

•To add each offer separately, in the 1st offer 2nd offer etc fields, enter the information you want and press the save button.
