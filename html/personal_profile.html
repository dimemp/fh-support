•To change your details, go to your profile. In the info tab, we can edit any field we are interested in. As soon as the editing is complete, press the save button.

•To link our linkedin account, go to your profile and in the field titled linked in, paste to URL of your linkedin account. Finally, click save.

•To write a short bio, go to your profile and in the section titled Short bio, write the text you want. Finally, press the save button.

•To change your password, go to your profile and select the settings tab. Enter the password you want and then select the Change password button.
